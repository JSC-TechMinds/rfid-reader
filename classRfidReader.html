String RfidReader::getSerialNumber ( )

Get the reader serial number.

This method reads the card number within the reader factory, a total of eight digits. Section 1 to 2 for the year of manufacture, the first 3 to 4 means weeks, the 5 to 8 digits are sequence number, 0001 to 9999.

Returns
String reader serial number.
